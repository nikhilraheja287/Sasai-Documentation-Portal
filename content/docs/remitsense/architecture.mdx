## Recommended shape

- Your frontend talks to your backend, not directly to Sasai secrets.
- Your backend authenticates requests to Sasai APIs.
- Your webhook consumer handles asynchronous product events.

## Design notes

- Keep credentials in a dedicated configuration layer.
- Log request identifiers, not sensitive headers.
- Isolate webhook verification and retry logic from business logic.
